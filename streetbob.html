Harley Davidson Street Bob

The Harley-Davidson Street Bob aims to give the thrills of motorcycle riding in bare-bones package. This cruiser bike is sure to give you a two-fists-in-the-wind riding experience with its pace and equipment.

For that tough and sporty look, this bike sports a number of blacked out-surfaces on its body, it also comes with a pair of spoked wheels, a digital instrument panel, a USB charging port, keyless ignition, LED headlight, and chopped mudguards.

Powering the Street Bob is a 1,745cc air-cooled Milwaukee Eight 107 engine that has two cylinders and four valves, producing a healthy 145Nm of torque. This mill is then mated to a 6-speed manual transmission.

For safety, it comes standard with an Anti-lock Braking System (ABS), immobilizer, slipper clutch, and an alarm system.
