Benelli Motorcycles

Benelli is a Chinese Italian motorcycle manufacturer which finds its roots all the way back in 1911. Fully owned by the Qianjiang Group, Benelli manufactures premium mid-displacement motorcycles, and sells them across several markets across the globe. Brand new Benelli motorcycles get the DNA and history of the iconic Italian company, with modern day innovations, cutting edge designs, and competitive market offerings. Benelli was established in Pesaro, Italy in 1911, which possibly makes it the oldest of Italian motorcycle factories in operation.
