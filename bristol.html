Bristol Motorcycles

Bristol is a Manila-based boutique motorcycle brand that builds vintage-styled European motorcycles for all kinds of riders. The brand takes pride in its lineup of unique motorcycles, which are a mashup from different countries, featuring Italian-inspired design, electronics from an American company, technical components from Japan, and powertrain from Taiwan. The brand debuted in the local market in 2018 and was founded under the philosophy that motorcycle riding should focus on fun. Bristol also believes that motorcycles should extend the owner’s personality through easy customization of their machines. Sinski Motorcycle Philippines is the official distributor since the brand entered the market.
